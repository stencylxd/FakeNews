Share the Secret Ingredient: Strawberries

There are many pick-your-own farms where I live that grow strawberries.
Strawberries are a very sweet and healthy ingredient that can top most desserts
or some meals like breakfast or lunch. They are many strawberries used where I live.

What are strawberries used in?

You can find them as a built in topping at most ice cream places or even just for sale as a snack.
You can also find strawberry-flavored things, but they really go best by themselves because they don't
really work with most dishes. Strawberry is mostly used as a flavor however.
